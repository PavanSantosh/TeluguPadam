Who I am

I'm Pavan Santhosh — a Wikimedian, community builder, writer, language activist, and amateur researcher. My work lives at the intersection of language, identity, and open knowledge.

I believe languages are not just tools of communication — they are living archives of culture, thought, and memory. Much of my energy goes into ensuring Telugu and other Indian languages thrive in the digital age, on Wikipedia and beyond.

I build communities not as a means to an end, but because I believe collective intelligence moves the world forward. Whether editing Wikipedia, organising contributors, or writing about dialects — everything comes from the same root: love.
